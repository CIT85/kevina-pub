Japanese Culture

Japan is a country with a rich and interesting history, and a culture very different from the western world. They are most known for their cherry blossoms and the infamous Mount Fuji, as well as some things like as karaoke and vending machines. Japan is full of good food like raman and sushi, good entertainment like anime and manga and great relaxtion spots the locals often go to like the hot springs and bath houses. Japan is also known for their formalities and respectfulness, as well as their beautiful architecture and landmarks.
